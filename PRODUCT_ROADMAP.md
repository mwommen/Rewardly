# Credit Card Optimizer Product Roadmap

## Vision

Make the credit card optimizer a practical wallet coach and checkout companion: help users pick the best card for every purchase, keep their benefits active, and surface the right card or signup offer exactly when they need it.

## Strategic themes

1. **Real-time checkout benefit alerts**
   - Detect merchants and show the best card for the current purchase.
   - Surface enrollment-required perks and one-time statements credits.

2. **Wallet intelligence**
   - Recommend the best existing card in the user's wallet.
   - Reveal coverage gaps, high-value categories, and underutilized benefits.

3. **Offer discovery**
   - Show merchant-specific offers and signup bonuses.
   - Help users compare benefit matches and apply to the best card.

4. **Retention workflows**
   - Track enrollments, reminders, and card usage triggers.
   - Remind users before benefits expire or annual fees become due.

## Sprint 1: Foundation + higher-value UX

### Goals

- Surface matching merchant offers and the best wallet card for purchases.
- Improve the recommendation experience with a clearer benefit library.
- Start building a roadmap that links product features to user value.

### Deliverables

- `frontend-vite` merchant search now shows both top rewards matches and additional merchant offers.
- The extension displays a stronger recommendation callout when shopping online.
- A documented product roadmap anchored in the current app/extension capabilities.

### Quick wins

- Merchant benefit search with a separate offer library.
- Wallet recommendation note that highlights exact match confidence and verification status.
- Basic product messaging for "best card in your wallet" and "other offers nearby."

## Sprint 2: Wallet coaching

### Goals

- Add wallet health summary and missed benefit alerts.
- Introduce "keep vs cancel" guidance for cards with low value.
- Add a simple onboarding flow for linking cards and tracking enrollments.

### Deliverables

- Wallet health dashboard with category coverage and active benefit counts.
- Reminder cards for upcoming enrollments and service credits.
- New backend support for card status and benefit reminders.
- UI coaching panel for wallet health, enrolled credits, and bulk reminder actions.
- Extension checkout banner that surfaces wallet credit counts and quick wallet coach access.

## Sprint 3: Personalized recommendations

### Goals

- Make recommendations personal based on spend, goals, and linked accounts.
- Add deeper benefit matching for travel, dining, and subscription categories.
- Build the foundation for premium experiences and AI-assisted decision support.

### Deliverables

- Spend profile inputs and personalized ranking in the app.
- AI-style guidance for which card to use, when to apply, and when to cancel.
- Enhanced card catalog filtering by issuer, annual fee, and reward type.

## Next steps

1. Validate the new merchant offer library UI with the current frontend.
2. Build the first wallet health summary panel in the dashboard.
3. Add enrollment / reminder state management in backend and UI.
4. Expand catalog coverage for more issuers and benefit categories.

---

> This roadmap is intentionally lightweight so we can move quickly from the current card catalog / extension foundation to a stronger product narrative and higher-impact features.
